💉 Sharps Disposal

UN-approved sharps containers and scheduled collections for clinical, medical, and domestic use — fully compliant with UK hazardous waste regulations.

What We Provide

Sharps waste — including needles, syringes, lancets, and blades — represents one of the highest-risk waste streams for injury and infection. Our sharps disposal service provides UN-approved, colour-coded sharps containers and scheduled collections to ensure safe handling from point of use to final disposal.

Whether you need single-use sharps bins or a regular exchange programme, we offer flexible solutions to suit clinical environments, home users, and businesses of any size.

Sharps Waste We Collect

  • Needles and syringes (yellow-lidded containers)
  • Medicinally contaminated sharps
  • Non-medicinally contaminated sharps
  • Lancets and finger-prick devices
  • Scalpel blades and surgical instruments
  • Glass ampoules and vials

Who This Service Is For

  • GP surgeries and NHS clinics
  • Diabetic and self-injection patients (domestic collections)
  • Aesthetics, tattooing, and piercing studios
  • Veterinary practices
  • Pharmacies and dispensaries
  • Occupational health services

Why Choose Apex for Sharps Disposal?

  • UN-approved, colour-coded sharps containers supplied
  • Containers available in multiple sizes (0.5L to 30L)
  • Scheduled exchanges or one-off collections available
  • All sharps treated as hazardous waste — no landfill disposal
  • Full compliance with Health & Safety at Work regulations
  • Domestic collection service available for home sharps users
