🦷 Dental Waste

Specialist dental waste collection for practices of all sizes — amalgam, clinical, and pharmaceutical dental waste handled compliantly and discreetly.

What We Provide

Dental practices generate a unique range of waste streams that require specialist handling. Our dental waste service covers amalgam waste, clinical dental waste, pharmaceutical returns, and general hygiene waste — all collected, transported, and disposed of in full compliance with UK and EU regulations.

We supply clearly labelled, purpose-built containers for each waste stream and arrange collections at a frequency that suits your practice schedule. You receive full documentation for every collection, ensuring your practice maintains a complete compliance audit trail.

Dental Waste Types Covered

  • Dental amalgam waste (fillings, capsules, separator sludge)
  • Clinical dental waste including contaminated instruments
  • Pharmaceutical and medicine waste
  • X-ray processing chemicals (fixer and developer)
  • Sharps generated during dental procedures
  • General infectious waste from the surgery

Who This Service Is For

  • NHS and private dental practices
  • Orthodontic and specialist dental clinics
  • Dental laboratories
  • Dental schools and training facilities

  • Fully compliant with Environment Agency and local authority requirements
  • Scheduled collections that fit around your appointments
  • Complete waste transfer documentation provided
