🏥 Clinical & Medical Waste

Safe, compliant removal of infectious and non-infectious healthcare waste. Secure containers, scheduled collections, and full traceability to meet all regulatory standards.

What We Provide

Our clinical and medical waste disposal service ensures that all healthcare waste — from infectious materials to pharmaceutical waste — is handled safely, legally, and responsibly. We supply secure, colour-coded containers appropriate to each waste stream and arrange scheduled collections that minimise disruption to your facility.

Every collection is accompanied by full documentation including waste transfer notes, giving your practice complete audit trails for regulatory compliance. Whether you generate small volumes of waste or require high-frequency collections, we tailor our service around your specific operational needs.

Waste Types Covered

  • Infectious and potentially infectious clinical waste
  • Non-infectious clinical and healthcare waste
  • Pharmaceutical and cytotoxic waste
  • Anatomical and pathological waste
  • Tiger-stripe (offensive) hygiene waste
  • Medicinally contaminated waste
  • Personal protective equipment (PPE) from clinical use

Who This Service Is For

  • GP surgeries and NHS practices
  • Private hospitals and clinics
  • Care homes and nursing facilities
  • Aesthetics and cosmetic clinics
  • Physiotherapy and podiatry practices
  • Veterinary surgeries
  • Pharmacies handling returned medicines

Why Choose Apex for Clinical Waste?

  • Fully licensed UK waste carrier with all legal documentation in order
  • Colour-coded, UN-approved clinical waste containers supplied
  • Scheduled or on-demand collections — we work around your hours
  • Waste transfer notes provided for every collection
  • All waste treated in accordance with the Hazardous Waste Regulations
  • Dedicated account manager and responsive customer support
