Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how Apex Hygiene UK collects, uses, and protects your personal data.

Last updated: January 2025

1. Introduction

CCS Group NW Limited, trading as Apex Hygiene UK ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting and respecting your privacy. This policy sets out the basis on which any personal data we collect from you, or that you provide to us, will be processed by us. Please read it carefully.

2. Data We Collect

We may collect and process the following data about you:

  • Information you provide when completing forms on our website (name, email, phone, business details)
  • Records of any correspondence if you contact us
  • Details of your visits to our website and the resources you access
  • Technical data including IP address, browser type, and operating system

3. How We Use Your Data

We use the information we hold about you to:

  • Provide our waste and hygiene services to you
  • Process your enquiries and respond to requests for quotes
  • Notify you about changes to our services
  • Ensure our website content is presented effectively
  • Comply with legal and regulatory obligations

4. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your data on the basis of: contract performance (to fulfil a service agreement), legitimate interests (to run our business effectively), legal obligation (to comply with regulations), and consent (where you have given us permission).

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share data with trusted third-party service providers who assist us in operating our website and delivering our services, subject to confidentiality agreements.

6. Data Retention

We retain personal data for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes we collected it for, including for the purposes of satisfying any legal, accounting, or reporting requirements. Generally, client data is retained for 6 years after the end of a contract.

7. Your Rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to: access your personal data, correct inaccurate data, request deletion of your data, object to processing, and data portability. To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at hello@apexhygieneuk.co.uk.
