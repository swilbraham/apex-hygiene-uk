Cookie Policy

This policy explains how we use cookies and similar tracking technologies on the Apex Hygiene UK website.

Last updated: January 2025

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and to provide information to the website owner.

How We Use Cookies

We use cookies to:

  • Ensure our website functions correctly (strictly necessary cookies)
  • Remember your preferences and settings
  • Understand how visitors interact with our website (analytics)
  • Improve the performance and usability of our site

Types of Cookies We Use

Strictly Necessary Cookies: These are essential for the website to operate. They cannot be switched off. They are usually set in response to actions you take, such as filling in a form.

Analytics Cookies: These allow us to count visits and understand how visitors move around our website. All information is aggregated and anonymous. We use this to improve how our website works.

Preference Cookies: These enable our website to remember information you have entered or choices you have made (such as your preferred language).

Managing Cookies

You can control and/or delete cookies as you wish. You can delete all cookies that are already on your computer and set most browsers to prevent them from being placed. However, if you do this, you may have to manually adjust some preferences each time you visit a site.

For more information about managing cookies, visit www.aboutcookies.org.
