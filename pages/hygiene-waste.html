♻️ Hygiene Waste

General hygiene waste management and collection for commercial and public premises — sustainable, compliant, and hassle-free.

What We Provide

Beyond specialist waste streams, most commercial premises generate a steady volume of general hygiene waste that still requires compliant, responsible disposal. Our hygiene waste service covers all offensive and non-infectious hygiene waste — collected regularly, disposed of sustainably, and documented fully.

We work with you to identify the right containers, appropriate waste segregation methods, and a collection schedule that keeps your premises tidy and your compliance up to date.

Hygiene Waste We Collect

  • Offensive/tiger-stripe hygiene waste
  • PPE and single-use protective equipment
  • Contaminated paper and hygiene products
  • Used gloves and aprons (non-clinical)
  • Feminine hygiene products
  • Wound dressings and bandages (non-infectious)

Who This Service Is For

  • Commercial offices and workplaces
  • Retail outlets and shopping centres
  • Hotels, restaurants, and hospitality venues
  • Sports and leisure facilities
  • Educational institutions
  • Manufacturing and industrial sites

Why Choose Apex for Hygiene Waste?

  • Suitable containers supplied and maintained
  • Waste segregation advice to reduce costs and improve compliance
  • Nationwide collection coverage
  • Environmentally responsible disposal methods prioritised
  • Transparent pricing and flexible contracts
  • Complete waste transfer documentation for all collections
