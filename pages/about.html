About Apex Hygiene UK

Building cleaner, safer, and more hygienic environments for businesses across every sector — with a strong focus on reliability, compliance, and customer care.

Who We Are

Hygiene isn't just a service — it's our promise.

At Apex Hygiene UK, we're committed to creating cleaner, safer, and more hygienic environments for businesses across every sector. With a strong focus on reliability, compliance, and customer care, we provide specialist waste management and hygiene services that help organisations maintain the highest standards of health and safety.

We understand that every business has unique needs, which is why we offer tailored solutions — ranging from clinical and medical waste disposal to sanitary, sharps, dental, nappy, and general hygiene waste services.

Our team is fully trained, accredited, and dedicated to handling all waste streams securely, responsibly, and in accordance with UK regulations.

What sets us apart is our customer-first approach. We pride ourselves on responsive communication, flexible collection schedules, and a level of service that puts your peace of mind at the centre of everything we do.

Whether you operate a healthcare facility, dental practice, childcare centre, commercial building, or public space — we're here to keep your environment clean, compliant, and cared for.
